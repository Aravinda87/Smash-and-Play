Techniques

A technique is a specific way through which one executes a task. The importance of technique comes from the efficiency of completing that task through a specific method. Anyone can throw a ball. But the right technique will help you throw the ball the farthest with the least amount of effort. Therefore, it is important to learn the various techniques in badminton in order to not over exert your body and/or endure injuries. Be it smashing or serving, knowing the right method is the key to ensure a smooth and enjoyable game.
